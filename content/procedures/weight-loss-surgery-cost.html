Cost

The cost of surgery includes Private hospital, Surgical and Anaesthetist charge. Please note that there will be additional costing if a repair of hiatal hernia, or if you have had previous weight loss surgery ( "Redo surgery").

Procedure Cost
Laparoscopic Sleeve Gastrectomy $24,000 *
Laparoscopic Roux-en-Y Gastric Bypass $25,500 - $26,500 *

* These costs are approximate.

Costs does not include pre and post-operative consultation with Mr Barnaby Smith, Bariatric Nurse specialist, Dietician and Psychologist. (approximately 16 appointments). Preoperative Very Low Calorie Diet (VLDC i.e. Optifast) and postoperative multivitamins also not included (Total additional $3,500).
